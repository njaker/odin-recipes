Dadar Telor Recipe

Dadar Telor (Omelette, eng): Simple, Delicious, and Always Tempting

Dadar Telor, a simple dish made from beaten eggs and fried, is a perfect choice for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. With a little creativity, an omelette can be a delicious and nutritious dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 scallion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced (optional)
  • 1 clove of garlic, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt, MSG, Pepper, cooking oil to taste

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper powder, whisk well.
  2. Add scallions, red chilies, and garlic (if using), stir well.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a non-stick pan.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, cook over medium heat until the bottom is cooked.
  5. Flip the omelette, cook until both sides are cooked and golden brown.
  6. Remove the omelette, serve while warm.

Enjoy a Special Simple Dadar Telor

The Dadar Telor is proof that even simple dishes can feel special. With easy-to-find ingredients and practical preparation, you can enjoy a delicious and nutritious omelette anytime. Good luck and be creative with additional ingredients to your liking!
